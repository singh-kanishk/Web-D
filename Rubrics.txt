

5. Evaluation Rubrics for Part A (Home Page)**

| **Criteria**             | **Max Points** | **Description**                                                                                         |
|---------------------------|----------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Design and Layout**     | 10             | Header, footer, and main content should be visually appealing and follow a consistent theme.            |
| **Navigation Functionality** | 10             | All navigation links (e.g., to Posts, About pages) must work correctly.                                 |
| **Content Clarity**       | 5              | The homepage must have a clear title and a short description of the website.                           |
| **Responsive Design**     | 5              | The page should adjust properly to different screen sizes (mobile, tablet, desktop).                   |

---

### **6. Evaluation Rubrics for Part B (Posts List Page)**

| **Criteria**                | **Max Points** | **Description**                                                                                           |
|------------------------------|----------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Content Structure**        | 10             | Posts are displayed as a clear and organized list (e.g., using `<ul>` or `<div>` with proper spacing).    |
| **Navigation Functionality** | 10             | Links to the individual posts (Post 1, Post 2, etc.) must be functional.                                  |
| **Styling and Layout**       | 5              | The posts list should be styled appropriately (e.g., with CSS).                                           |
| **Responsive Design**        | 5              | The page layout should adjust correctly across various screen sizes.                                      |

---

### **7. Evaluation Rubrics for Part C (Post Page)**

| **Criteria**                | **Max Points** | **Description**                                                                                           |
|------------------------------|----------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Content Presentation**     | 10             | The post content (title, body) must be well-presented and clear.                                          |
| **Navigation Functionality** | 10             | Links to the homepage and posts list must be functional.                                                  |
| **Consistency**              | 5              | The design should match the style and theme of the Home Page and Posts List Page.                         |
| **Dynamic Behavior**         | 5              | If JavaScript is used, the post content should dynamically update based on the selected post (optional).  |

---

### How to Use These Rubrics
- Each rubric evaluates the implementation quality of different aspects of your web pages.
- Total possible points for each part are **30**.
- A perfect score indicates a well-designed, functional, and responsive page.
